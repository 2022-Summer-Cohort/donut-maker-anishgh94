Fred the Baking Company

A game that uses button clicks to create virtual donuts for the world to enjoy. Click on donuts to increase donut counts, use the donuts you generated to buy auto clickers and multipliers to speed up as well as automate the process of donut making. Buy enough auto clickers and multipliers and watch your number of donuts count go up.

Cookie Clicker

This little game is inspired by the game Cookie Clicker. Click on the name to go to the original game.
